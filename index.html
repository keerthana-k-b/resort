Lumière Obsidian Tropics sits upon a prehistoric volcanic outcrop. Every architectural element was crafted to honor the rugged black obsidian stone, balanced with the lightness of hand-woven palm and recycled teak.

Culinary Journey

Forged by Fire, Curated by Season.

At Lumière, gastronomy is an ephemeral craft. Led by Executive Chef Elena Vance, our kitchen works in complete dialogue with local organic growers and artisanal fishermen. We use volcanic stone grills to sear local reef lobster and black-grouper over open fire wood-hearths.

Every dish is paired with rare wines from our volcanic stone cellar, curated specifically to mirror the mineral depth of our volcanic terroir.

Sustainability

Off-Grid Volcanic Sanctuary

Lumière is designed to leave zero trace. Our sanctuary operates with 100% solar self-sufficiency, a dedicated organic water desalination system, and a strict plastic-free policy across the entire island. We also maintain a basalt coral nursery to restore the local marine lagoon.
